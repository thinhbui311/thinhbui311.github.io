He's not only good at combat, but he also rock the dance floor. U gotta see him dance honestly!
While Yakuza is a crime drama that often hits many of the beats associated with the genre, it is also a supremely weird series that constantly throws caution to the wind. Consequently, the perpetually calm Kiryu can quickly go from stoically demolishing dozens of yakuza members to dancing the night away in a club.
