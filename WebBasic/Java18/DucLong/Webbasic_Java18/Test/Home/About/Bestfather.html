Kiryu is pretty clueless when it comes to most things, and that's part of what makes his character so endearing to fans. So what happens when you take a clueless guy and make him take care of a kid?
Well, there are definitely some bad decisions made... Kiryu takes young Haruka to the underground gambling den, a place where you can play minigames. Arguably, this isn't the worst place he takes her to, as he also takes her to a soapland.
